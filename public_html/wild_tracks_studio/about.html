About Wild Tracks Studio

Wild Tracks Studio is a small experiment with a big hope: that playing with bird sounds makes people curious about the birds themselves. Every sample in the studio is a real recording of a real species. If arranging a chickadee's call into a melody makes you look one up — or step outside and listen for one — this app has done its job.

Bird populations in North America have declined by roughly three billion birds since 1970. Awareness is the first step toward conservation. This project is part of Soaring Wild.
